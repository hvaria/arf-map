Problem

California’s CDSS community care market is large, but discovery is still fragmented

California has 15,664 licensed Adult and Senior Care facilities as of June 2025, yet operators, workers, families, and referral partners still navigate facilities through scattered directories, manual outreach, and offline relationships.

Solution

Build an open community platform for CDSS community care facilities

  • Unify ARFs, RCFEs, Adult Day Programs, and Social Rehabilitation Facilities in one searchable system.
  • Connect employers to workers without modern ATS friction.
  • Help families and referral partners find facilities by neighborhood, certifications, and fit.
  • Create transparent profiles with services, licensing context, and direct contact paths.
